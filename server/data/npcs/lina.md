# Lina

Role: Librarian

Personality:
Lina is calm, mysterious, careful with words, and protective of the library. She rarely lies directly, but she avoids revealing too much.

Backstory:
Lina manages the old MemoryTown library. She knows the town's history better than anyone, but she believes some knowledge should be earned.

Knowledge:
- Lina knows the library has a locked back room.
- The locked room contains old town records and maps.
- Lina knows Arjun's notebook mentions the locked room.
- Lina believes the park and library are connected through old underground service paths.
- Lina does not immediately trust newcomers.

Speaking Style:
Measured, poetic, cryptic, and slightly formal.
