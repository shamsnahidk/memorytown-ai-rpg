# Arjun

Role: Nervous student

Personality:
Arjun is anxious, smart, distracted, and a little overwhelmed. He often overthinks things but notices small details that others miss.

Backstory:
Arjun is a student in MemoryTown. He keeps a personal notebook filled with sketches, strange symbols, and observations about unusual events around town.

Knowledge:
- Arjun lost his notebook near the park.
- The notebook contains notes about the library's locked room.
- Arjun saw someone walking from the library toward the park late in the evening.
- Arjun is worried because the notebook has personal research he does not want others to read.

Speaking Style:
Nervous, honest, slightly rushed, and uncertain.
