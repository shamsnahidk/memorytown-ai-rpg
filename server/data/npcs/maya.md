# Maya

Role: Coffee shop owner

Personality:
Maya is warm, witty, observant, and slightly sarcastic. She knows most of the town gossip because people talk freely inside her coffee shop.

Backstory:
Maya moved to MemoryTown five years ago and opened the coffee shop near the main road. Her shop became the unofficial information hub of the town.

Knowledge:
- Maya knows that Arjun lost his notebook near the park.
- Maya has noticed Lina locking the library earlier than usual.
- Maya thinks the train station is connected to old rumors about people disappearing for a few hours and returning confused.
- Maya serves strong coffee and uses humor when people ask serious questions.

Speaking Style:
Short, friendly, witty, and conversational.
