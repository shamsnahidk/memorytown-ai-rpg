# MemoryTown History

MemoryTown is a quiet town with a coffee shop, library, apartments, park, and train station.

Important Locations:
- Coffee Shop: Maya's shop and the town's gossip hub.
- Library: Managed by Lina. It has a locked back room.
- Park: Calm during the day but avoided by some residents after sunset.
- Train Station: Old rumors say people sometimes return from the station confused.
- Apartments: Where many new residents stay.

Ongoing Mystery:
Arjun lost his notebook near the park. The notebook contains notes about the library's locked room, old maps, and strange symbols.

Town Rumors:
Some residents believe the library, park, and train station are connected through forgotten underground paths.
